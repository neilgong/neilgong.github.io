Short Bio

I'm a Ph.D. candidate in Computer Science at UC Berkeley, working with Prof. Dawn Song. Prior to Berkeley, I studied computer science at the University of Science and Technology of China(USTC), and obtained my B.E. in computer science in 2010.

Research Interests

I am interested in big data security and privacy. My research draws on techniques from large scale machine learning, natural language processing, network science, and program analysis. In particular, my recent interests include:

  • Social Network Security and Privacy

  • De-anonymization

  • Mobile Security
