About me

I obtained a Ph.D in Computer Science from UC Berkeley in May 2015. My advisor is Dawn Song. I earned my B.E. in computer science from the University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) in 2010. I obtained Guo Moruo Scholarship, the highest honor for undergraduate students at USTC.

I'm looking for motivated students. If you have interest, please contact me.

Research Interests

I am broadly interested in security, privacy, and their intersection with data science. In particular, I recently focus on:

  • Secure and Privacy-Preserving Social Media

  • Authentication/Access Control

  • Mobile Security and Privacy

  • Security and Privacy in Internet-of-Things
